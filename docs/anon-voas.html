David Voas data

This page deals with a study of 2007 conducted by David Voas using data from the UK Office for National Statistics (ONS) for the 2001 census in England and Wales.
He studied sun - sun relationships between wife and husband, for aroud 10 millions of couples.

References

  • voasastrology.pdf: Ten million marriages: A test of astrological ‘love signs’, a presentation of this study by David Voas, 2007.
  • Love not in the stars, a presentation of the study on Manchester University web site.
