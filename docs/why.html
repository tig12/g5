Why Gauquelin5 ?

I started this software because I'd like to answer a question :
Do the statistical tests done on astrology by Gauquelin and successors give significant results or not ?

A confuse situation

The topic is highly controversial and there are roughly three kinds conclusions :
  • Skeptics think that Gauquelin tests have been invalidated by counter-experiences lead by different groups of skeptics.
    The do not consider Suibert Ertel's re-analysis as significant.
  • Believers think that Gauquelin tests were reproduced and confirmed, that counter-experiences lead by skeptic groups were entached with fraud.
    They estimate that Suibert Ertel's re-analysis confirmed the Gauquelin's results.
  • Some people, like Geoffrey Dean, admit the existence of statistical effects, but think that they may have been generated by false declaration to civil registries.

But there must be an answer

A precision first : trying to find out if a statistical test is significant is a different question than trying to find out if astrology is true or not.
Said an other way : observing the presence or absence of a statistical effect is different from the interpretation of this effect.

So I think that there should be a clear answer to my question.

Documentation and open data

To reach a consensus about the question, I think that current and next generations need to re-examine the whole history. Reproduce the tests, document the arguments of Gauquelin, Ertel, skeptics to permit professional statisticians to study the question.

My opinion is that open data and free software are necessary.
