Gauquelin anonymous data

Besides collecting birth dates of famous persons, Michel and Françoise Gauquelin gathered thousands of anonymous birth dates.
The particularity of Gauquelin anonymous data is to contain birth times (Sachs, Castille and Voas data only contain birth days).
